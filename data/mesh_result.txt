
1: Brain Diseases
Pathologic conditions affecting the BRAIN, which is composed of the intracranial 
components of the CENTRAL NERVOUS SYSTEM. This includes (but is not limited to)
the CEREBRAL CORTEX; intracranial white matter; BASAL GANGLIA; THALAMUS;
HYPOTHALAMUS; BRAIN STEM; and CEREBELLUM.
